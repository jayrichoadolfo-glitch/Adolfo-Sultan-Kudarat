Lutayan: The Lakeside Living

Ancestral heartland and home to the serene waters of Lake Buluan.

Ancestral Roots

Lutayan is the ancestral home of the Mangudadatu clan and is centered around the life-giving waters of Lake Buluan. The culture here is deeply tied to the lake, which has sustained and shaped the identity of the community for generations.

The Highlight

Lake Buluan is renowned for producing the cleanest-tasting freshwater Tilapia in the country. The lake is vast, serene, and offers sunset views that rival any coastal destination, making it a prime spot for reflection, photography, and culinary exploration.

The Vibe

Spiritual and calm. Life in Lutayan moves at the pace of a paddle-boat on the water, offering a peaceful retreat from the bustle of the commercial centers and inviting visitors to embrace a slower, more intentional way of living.
