Lambayong: The Rice Granary

The green heart of Sultan Kudarat fueled by the mighty Allah River.

Background

If Sultan Kudarat is the "Breadbasket" of the region, Lambayong is its heart. This municipality is defined by the mighty Allah River, which feeds the vast irrigation systems that keep the province productive and green year-round.

The Highlight

The Allah River System is the lifeblood of the town. During the golden hour, the river reflects the shifting colors of the sky, providing a stunning and peaceful backdrop for the endless, swaying rice fields that stretch toward the horizon.

The Vibe

Rural, scenic, and deeply tranquil. Lambayong is the perfect destination for a long, slow road trip through the countryside, where the landscape tells the story of the province’s rich agricultural heritage.
