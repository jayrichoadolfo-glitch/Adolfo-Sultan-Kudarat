Columbio The Green Ridge

Home to the breathtaking La Palmera Mountain Ridge. The landscape is a series of endless, rolling green hills that offer a 360-degree view of the province. It is often called the "Little New Zealand" of Mindanao.

Background: Bordering Davao del Sur and North Cotabato, Columbio is a highland territory that was once a quiet agricultural town.

The Vibe: It has exploded in popularity due to La Palmera Ridge. It feels like a "green sea," where the wind never stops and the views go on forever.

Quick Info

Highlight: La Palmera Ridge.

Terrain: Rolling Hills.

Best For: Camping & Photography.
