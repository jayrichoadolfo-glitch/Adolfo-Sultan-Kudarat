Geography & Climate

Discover the dramatic landscapes of the Golden Heart of Mindanao.

The province is a geographical masterpiece of over 5,000 square kilometers. To the West, the coastline touches the Celebes Sea, guarded by the rugged Daguma Mountain Range. To the East, the land opens into the fertile Allah Valley, a vast agricultural basin.

This "Wall of Mountains" creates a natural barrier that protects the inland plains, resulting in a climate that is perfect for both coastal fishing and inland farming. The rainfall is evenly distributed throughout the year (Type IV Climate), keeping the land lush and productive.
