Tacurong City: The Goodwill City

The beating heart of commerce and the province's primary eco-tourism gateway.

The Urban Sanctuary

The only city in the province and its primary commercial hub, Tacurong is home to the world-renowned Baras Bird Sanctuary. This eco-tourism site hosts over 20,000 birds, including Egrets and Herons, within a protected habitat that was once a humble peppercorn farm.

Background

Originally a vital crossroad for regional traders, Tacurong attained cityhood in 2000. It now serves as the central hub of trade and the primary entrance for travelers coming from neighboring provinces into the heart of Sultan Kudarat.

The Vibe

Tacurong perfectly balances rapid urban development with a deep commitment to environmental preservation. The city offers a unique atmosphere where modern progress and the quietude of nature coexist, providing a peaceful refuge for wildlife and urban explorers alike.
