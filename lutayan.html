Lutayan The Lakeside Living

Background: Lutayan is the ancestral home of the Mangudadatu clan and is centered around the life-giving waters of Lake Buluan. The culture here is deeply tied to the lake, which has sustained the community for generations.

The Highlight: Lake Buluan. Known for producing the cleanest-tasting freshwater Tilapia in the country. The lake is vast, serene, and offers sunset views that rival any coastal destination.

The Vibe: Spiritual and calm. Life moves at the pace of a paddle-boat on the water.

Quick Info

Activity: Lakeside Dining & Boating.

Must-Eat: Grilled Tilapia (Nilasing na Tilapia).

Best Time: Late Afternoon (Sunset).
