Bagumbayan The Agri-Adventure Hub

Background: What started as a humble resettlement area for farmers has transformed into the "Adventure Capital" of the province. Bagumbayan sits at a high elevation, offering some of the most dramatic views in Central Mindanao.

The Highlight: Bansada Agri-Eco Park. On a clear morning, the park is engulfed in a "Sea of Clouds." It features one of the longest ziplines in the region, soaring over fruit orchards and coffee plantations.

The Vibe: It feels like a mountain retreat. The air is crisp, and the landscape is dominated by pine trees and organized agriculture.

Quick Info

Altitude: High (Cool Climate).

Best Time: 5:00 AM (for the Sea of Clouds).

Activity: Ziplining & Sky-biking.
