Columbio

The Green Ridge: Discover the "Little New Zealand" of Mindanao.

Background

Bordering Davao del Sur and North Cotabato, Columbio is a highland territory that was once a quiet agricultural town. Its unique elevation provides a cooler climate and a terrain unlike anywhere else in the province, characterized by rolling hills that seem to go on forever.

The Highlight: La Palmera Mountain Ridge

Home to the breathtaking La Palmera Mountain Ridge. The landscape is a series of endless, rolling green hills that offer a 360-degree view of the province. It is often called the "Little New Zealand" of Mindanao because of its emerald slopes and vast open spaces.

The Vibe

It has exploded in popularity due to the ridge, feeling like a "green sea" where the wind never stops. It's the ultimate destination for those who want to disconnect from the digital world and immerse themselves in the raw beauty of nature.
