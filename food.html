Explore Your Taste

A Halal-friendly blend of Maguindanaon spices and Visayan traditions.

Pastil

The "National Breakfast" of the region. Steamed rice topped with sautéed shredded chicken (Kagikit) wrapped in a fragrant banana leaf.

Tinagtag

A crispy, golden-brown snack made of ground rice and sugar, looking like a delicate mesh of fried noodles. Essential for festivals.

SNA Coffee

Award-winning high-altitude Robusta and Arabica beans sourced directly from the misty mountains of Senator Ninoy Aquino.
