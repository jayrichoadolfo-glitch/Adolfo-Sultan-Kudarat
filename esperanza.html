Esperanza The Land of Hot & Cold

Background: Esperanza is a sanctuary of natural wellness. Its name, Spanish for "Hope," reflects the healing nature of its landscapes. It is situated at the foot of the Daguma Mountain Range, where the earth’s geothermal energy meets mountain spring water.

The Highlight: Marquez Hot and Cold Springs. This is a rare geological phenomenon where a blistering hot spring and a freezing cold spring emerge just meters apart.The Adventure:

The Adventure: It is also the gateway to the Panigan Underground River, a mystical turquoise waterway that flows beneath limestone arches.

Quick Info

Activity: Natural Hydrotherapy & Spelunking.

Entry Fee: ₱20 – ₱50.

Vibe: Relaxing / Wellness.
