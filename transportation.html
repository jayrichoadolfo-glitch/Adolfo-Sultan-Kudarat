Transportation

Sultan Kudarat is the central gateway of South Central Mindanao. The city of Tacurong serves as the primary "Grand Central Terminal" where all major land routes intersect. Whether you are coming from Davao City, General Santos, or Cotabato City, you will likely pass through here.

Primary Hubs: Tacurong City and Isulan are the main terminals.

Major Bus Lines: * Yellow Bus Line (YBL): Connects to GenSan and Davao.

Husky Bus: Connects to Cotabato City and General Santos.

The Coastal Route: Explain the Sen. Ninoy Aquino (SNA) mountain road. It is a scenic, winding highway that has cut travel time to Lebak and Kalamansig by hours.

Local Transport: * The "Skylab": An iconic modified motorcycle with wooden wings for balance, used to reach mountain ridges like Columbio.

Tricycles: The "King of the Road" for short distances within town centers.

Quick Info

Hub: Tacurong Public Terminal.

From GenSan: 1.5 - 2 Hours.

From Davao: 4 - 5 Hours.

Local Tip: Take the "Executive Class" buses for air-conditioning and faster travel.
