Sen. Ninoy Aquino

The Coffee Frontier & Archaeological Heart of Sultan Kudarat.

Background

Formed in 1989 and tucked high in the Daguma Range, this municipality represents the true "frontier" of the province. Its misty, cool climate is ideal for producing some of the Philippines' finest Robusta and Arabica beans.

The Vibe

Life here moves at the pace of a slow-brewed cup of coffee. The air is always cool and smells of roasting beans and damp earth—a perfect escape from the heat of the plains. Beyond agriculture, it is a site of great archaeological importance due to the prehistoric Kulaman Burial Jars found in its many limestone caves.
