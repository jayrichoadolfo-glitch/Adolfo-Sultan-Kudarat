Tacurong City: The Goodwill City

The only city in the province and the primary commercial hub. It is home to the Baras Bird Sanctuary, a world-class eco-tourism site where over 20,000 birds—including Egrets and Herons—roost in a protected former peppercorn farm.

Background

Originally a crossroad for traders, Tacurong became a city in 2000 and has since served as the "beating heart" of commerce in the province. It serves as the gateway to Sultan Kudarat for travelers coming from neighboring provinces.

The Vibe

Tacurong perfectly balances urban development with environmental preservation. The Baras Bird Sanctuary is a testament to how nature and urban progress can coexist, providing a peaceful refuge for both wildlife and visitors.
