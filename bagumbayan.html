Bagumbayan

The Agri-Adventure Capital of the Golden Heart.

Background

What started as a humble resettlement area for farmers has transformed into the "Adventure Capital" of the province. Bagumbayan sits at a high elevation, offering some of the most dramatic views in Central Mindanao.

The Highlight: Bansada Agri-Eco Park

This park is the crown jewel of the municipality. On a clear morning, visitors are treated to a breathtaking "Sea of Clouds" that engulfs the valleys below. For thrill-seekers, it features one of the longest ziplines in the region, soaring over sprawling fruit orchards and coffee plantations.

The Vibe

It feels like a true mountain retreat. The air is crisp, and the landscape is dominated by pine trees and organized agriculture. It is the go-to spot for those seeking a balance of adrenaline and high-altitude tranquility.

Quick Info

Altitude
