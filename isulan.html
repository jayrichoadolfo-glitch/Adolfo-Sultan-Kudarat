Isulan: The Golden Capital

Isulan is the proud administrative heart and the seat of the provincial government of Sultan Kudarat. Its name is derived from the local Maguindanaon expression "Isulan Niyan", which translates to "to progress" or "to change for the better".

At night, the entire Capitol complex transforms into a sea of lights, attracting photographers and families from all over the region who come to witness the golden glow against the dark Mindanao sky.
