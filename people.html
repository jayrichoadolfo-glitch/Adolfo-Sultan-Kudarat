People & Culture

Sultan Kudarat is the ultimate "Melting Pot". It is defined by the Tri-People concept: the indigenous Lumads (Manobo and Teduray), the Muslim Filipinos (Maguindanaon), and the Christian Settlers (Ilonggo, Ilocano, and Cebuano).

Peace is not just a word here; it is the "Kalimudan" spirit—the act of gathering and uniting despite different beliefs and origins.
