Palimbang The Virgin Paradise

Background: Palimbang is for the "true" traveler. Located at the southernmost tip of the province, it remains one of the most untouched coastal areas in the Philippines. It has a deep history as a center of Islamic culture and maritime trade.

The Highlight: Alidama Island. A crescent-shaped island with blindingly white sand and turquoise waters. There are no luxury resorts here—just raw, natural beauty and world-class snorkeling.

The Vibe: Remote, quiet, and pristine. It is the "Last Frontier" of Sultan Kudarat.

Quick Info

Status: Off-the-beaten-path.

Activity: Snorkeling & Deep-sea Fishing.

Note: Coordinate with the local tourism office for security and boat rentals.
