Palimbang: The Virgin Paradise

Experience the raw beauty of Sultan Kudarat's southernmost maritime frontier.

Background

Palimbang is for the "true" traveler. Located at the southernmost tip of the province, it remains one of the most untouched coastal areas in the Philippines. It holds a deep history as a significant center of Islamic culture and maritime trade.

The Highlight

Alidama Island is the crown jewel—a crescent-shaped island with blindingly white sand and turquoise waters. There are no luxury resorts here; just raw, natural beauty and world-class snorkeling in crystal-clear depths.

The Vibe

Remote, quiet, and pristine. It is the "Last Frontier" of Sultan Kudarat, offering a rare glimpse into Mindanao's wild and beautiful coastline where nature remains the primary architect.
