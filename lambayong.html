Lambayong The Rice Granary

Background: If Sultan Kudarat is the "Breadbasket" of the region, Lambayong is its heart. This municipality is defined by the mighty Allah River, which feeds the vast irrigation systems that keep the province green.

The Highlight: The Allah River System. During the golden hour (sunset), the river reflects the sky, creating a stunning backdrop for the endless rice fields.

The Vibe: Rural and scenic. It’s the perfect place for a long, slow road trip through the countryside.

Quick Info

Terrain: Vast Plains / Riverbanks.

Best For: Scenic Road Trips & Photography.

Primary Crop: Rice and Corn.
