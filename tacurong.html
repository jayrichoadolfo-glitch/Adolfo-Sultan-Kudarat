Tacurong City: The Goodwill City

The only city in the province and the primary commercial hub. It is home to the Baras Bird Sanctuary, a world-class eco-tourism site where over 20,000 birds—including Egrets and Herons—roost in a protected former peppercorn farm.

Background: The only city in the province, originally a crossroad for traders. It became a city in 2000 and is the "beating heart" of commerce.

The Vibe: Home to the Baras Bird Sanctuary, a former pepper farm where 20,000+ birds sought refuge—a testament to how nature and urban progress can coexist.

Quick Info

Highlight: Baras Bird Sanctuary.

Entry Fee: ₱20–₱50.

Activity: Bird Watching & Talakudong Festival.
