Transportation Guide

Sultan Kudarat is the central gateway of South Central Mindanao. The city of Tacurong serves as the primary "Grand Central Terminal" where all major land routes intersect.

Major Bus Lines

  • Yellow Bus Line (YBL): Connects the province to General Santos City and Davao City.
  • Husky Bus: The primary link to Cotabato City and General Santos.

Primary Hubs

Tacurong City and Isulan are the main terminals. Whether you are coming from Davao, GenSan, or Cotabato, you will likely pass through these transit hearts.

The Coastal Route

The Sen. Ninoy Aquino (SNA) mountain road is a marvel of engineering. This scenic, winding highway through the Daguma Range has cut travel time to the coastal towns of Lebak and Kalamansig by hours, replacing the old, long sea voyages.

Local Transport

The "Skylab"
An iconic modified motorcycle with wooden "wings" for balance. These are essential for reaching remote mountain ridges like those in Columbio.

Tricycles
The "King of the Road" for short distances. Every town has a unique tricycle design suited to its specific terrain.
